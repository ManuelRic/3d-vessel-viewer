General:
    Right-click and drag to rotate the camera
    Hold Shift and right-drag to rotate around the center

On Vessel:
    Hover over a vessel to show its name
    Left-click a vessel to open its detail card
    Double-click a vessel to focus and follow it

On Empty:
    Click to restore the default view (close details + focused elements) ?
    Drag to move the camera across the map

On Trail:
    Hover to show the corresponding vessel name
    Click to elevate the trail y axis + highlight trail
    Double-click to dim other trails
